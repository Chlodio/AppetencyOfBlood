Adam I (962–1015)

1000–1015

  • Elected

Adam was born around 962.
In 979, at the age of sixteen he married fourteen-year-old Isabella, the union lasted 36 years, and 47 days, which resulted in two sons and six daughters; their first child, Adam II was born in 980, the surviving children were: Adam II, Alice, Ailova, Peter, Emma, and Isabella.
In 1000 he ascended the throne at the age of thirty-seven and reigned the next 15 years and 52 days. Adam I was the founder of long-lasting Shrewsbury dynasty.

Adam II (980–1039)

1015–1039

  • Son of Adam I

Adam was born in 6 January 980 as the firstborn son of seventeen-year-old Adam I and fifteen-year-old Isabella.
In 997, at the age of sixteen he married his cousin thirteen-year-old Edith Wragby, firstborn daughter of Reginald Wragby, the union lasted 34 years, and 209 days, which resulted in two sons and one daughter; their first child, Emma, 1st Princess was born in 998, the surviving children were: Emma, Adam III, and Peter I. In 1034, at the age of fifty-four he married his cousin sixteen-year-old Agnes Brighouse, third daughter of Michael Brighouse, the union lasted five years, and 88 days, but the union proved childless for Adam failed to impregnate his wife.
In 1015 he ascended the throne at the age of thirty-five and reigned the next 24 years and 146 days. Adam II was the 2nd ruler from Shrewsbury dynasty.

Adam III (1009–1059)

1039–1059

  • Son of Adam II

Adam was born in 10 August 1009 as the firstborn son of twenty-nine-year-old Adam II, firstborn son of Adam I and twenty-six-year-old Edith Wragby, firstborn daughter of Reginald Wragby.
In 1024, at the age of fifteen he married thirty-nine-year-old Eunice, the union lasted 18 years, and 14 days, which resulted in one daughter; their only child, Alice, 1st Princess was born in 1030. In 1044, at the age of thirty-four he married sixteen-year-old Ragenild Stratton, second daughter of Henry Stratton, the union lasted 15 years, and 33 days, but the union proved childless for Adam failed to impregnate his wife.
In 1039 he ascended the throne at the age of twenty-nine and reigned the next 19 years and 302 days. Adam III was the 3rd ruler from Shrewsbury dynasty.

Peter I (1020–1080)

  • Son of Adam II
  • Brother of Adam III

Peter was born in 18 February 1020 as the youngest son of forty-year-old Adam II, firstborn son of Adam I and thirty-seven-year-old Edith Wragby, firstborn daughter of Reginald Wragby. At the time he had a brother, Adam III.
In 1036, at the age of sixteen he married fourteen-year-old Alice Helmsley, only daughter of Godfrey Helmsley, the union lasted 38 years, and 204 days, which resulted in two sons and two daughters; their first child, Alice Shrewsbury was born in 1039, the surviving children were: Peter II and Isabella. In 1075, at the age of fifty-five he married sixteen-year-old Matilda Kingsbridge, firstborn daughter of Simon Kingsbridge, the union lasted four years, and 177 days, but the union proved childless for Matilda was barren.
In 1059 he ascended the throne at the age of thirty-nine and reigned the next 20 years and 246 days. Peter I was the 4th ruler from Shrewsbury dynasty.
