Arnold I (965–1013)

    1000–1013

    • Elected

    He ascended the throne at the age of thirty-four and reigned the next 13 years and 299 days. In 990, at the age of twenty-four he married fourteen-year-old Masota, the union lasted 23 years, and 297 days.

    Arnold II (994–1051)

    1013–1051

    • Son of Arnold I

    He was born to twenty-eight-year-old Arnold I and eighteen-year-old Masota. He ascended the throne at the age of nineteen and reigned the next 37 years and 86 days. In 1009, at the age of fourteen he married thirty-eight-year-old Clarica, the union lasted 14 years, and 259 days. In 1028, at the age of thirty-four he married twenty-eight-year-old Denise Highbridge, oldest daughter of Denis Highbridge, the union lasted 22 years, and 34 days.
