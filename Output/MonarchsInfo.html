Walkelin (971–1023)

    1000–1023

    • Elected

    He ascended the throne at the age of twenty-eight and reigned the next 23 years and 307 days. In 986, at the age of fourteen he married the same age Lina, the union lasted 37 years, and 304 days; their first child, Walkelin was born in 993.

    Walkelin (993–1042)

    1023–1042

    • Son of Walkelin

    He was born to twenty-one-year-old Walkelin and twenty-one-year-old Lina. He ascended the throne at the age of thirty and reigned the next 18 years and 352 days. In 1008, at the age of fourteen he married the same age Gunnilda Felixstowe, oldest daughter of William Felixstowe, the union lasted 34 years, and 293 days; their first child, Estrild, 1st Princess was born in 1022.
