Jocelyn I (970–1022)

1000–1022

  • Elected

Jocelyn was born around 970.
In 991, at the age of twenty he married fourteen-year-old Estrild, the union lasted 31 years, and 134 days, which resulted in seven sons and one daughter; their first child, Jocelyn II was born in 993, the surviving children were: Jocelyn II, William, Alviva, and Ralph.
In 1000 he ascended the throne at the age of twenty-nine and reigned the next 22 years and 136 days. Jocelyn I was the founder of long-lasting Braintree dynasty.

Jocelyn II (993–1046)

1022–1046

  • Son of Jocelyn I

Jocelyn was born in 2 January 993 as the firstborn son of twenty-two-year-old Jocelyn I and sixteen-year-old Estrild.
In 1011, at the age of eighteen he married his cousin fifteen-year-old Matilda Hartland, firstborn daughter of Jordan Hartland, the union lasted 34 years, and 234 days, which resulted in four sons and five daughters; their first child, Ralph I was born in 1015, the surviving children were: Ralph I, Matilda, Alviva, Jocelyn III, Matilda, Alan, and William.
In 1022 he ascended the throne at the age of twenty-nine and reigned the next 24 years and 22 days. Jocelyn II was the 2nd ruler from Braintree dynasty.

Ralph I (1015–1068)

1046–1068

  • Son of Jocelyn II

Ralph was born in 14 February 1015 as the firstborn son of twenty-two-year-old Jocelyn II, firstborn son of Jocelyn I and nineteen-year-old Matilda Hartland, firstborn daughter of Jordan Hartland.
In 1033, at the age of eighteen he married twelve-year-old Edith Dover, third daughter of Bartholomew Dover, the union lasted 35 years, and 159 days, but the union proved childless for Ralph was incapable of breeding.
In 1046 he ascended the throne at the age of thirty-one and reigned the next 22 years and 188 days. Ralph I was the 3rd ruler from Braintree dynasty.

Jocelyn III (1025–1076)

1068–1076

  • Son of Jocelyn II
  • Brother of Ralph I

Jocelyn was born in 26 October 1025 as the second son of thirty-two-year-old Jocelyn II, firstborn son of Jocelyn I and twenty-nine-year-old Matilda Hartland, firstborn daughter of Jordan Hartland. At the time he had a brother, Ralph I.
In 1050, at the age of twenty-four he married thirteen-year-old Alice Beverley, firstborn daughter of Benedict Beverley, the union lasted 24 years, and four days, which resulted in five sons and three daughters; their first child, Jocelyn IV was born in 1057, the surviving children were: Jocelyn IV, Ralph, Alviva, and Avice. In 1075, at the age of forty-nine he married his niece fourteen-year-old Alice Braintree, firstborn daughter of Jocelyn III, the union lasted 220 days, but the union proved childless for Jocelyn failed to impregnate his wife. In 1076, at the age of fifty he married fifteen-year-old Alice Kington, second daughter of Peter Kington, the union lasted 66 days, but the union proved childless for Alice was barren.
In 1068 he ascended the throne at the age of forty-three and reigned the next seven years and 273 days. Jocelyn III was the 4th ruler from Braintree dynasty.

Jocelyn IV (1057–1114)

1076–1114

  • Son of Jocelyn III

Jocelyn was born in 25 July 1057 as the firstborn son of thirty-one-year-old Jocelyn III, second son of Jocelyn II and twenty-year-old Alice Beverley, firstborn daughter of Benedict Beverley.
In 1073, at the age of sixteen he married his second cousin thirteen-year-old Alice Hastings, second daughter of Peter Hastings, the union lasted 34 years, and 215 days, which resulted in one daughter; their only child, Alviva, 1st Princess was born in 1082, but she didn't survive. In 1108, at the age of fifty-one he married seventeen-year-old Emma Hastings, firstborn daughter of Ralph Hastings, the union lasted six years, and 36 days, which resulted in one son; their only child, Jocelyn V was born in 1111.
In 1076 he ascended the throne at the age of nineteen and reigned the next 37 years and 357 days. Jocelyn IV was the 5th ruler from Braintree dynasty.

Jocelyn V (1111–1167)

1114–1167

  • Son of Jocelyn IV

Jocelyn was born in 20 February 1111 as the only son of fifty-three-year-old Jocelyn IV, firstborn son of Jocelyn III and the firstborn son of twenty-year-old Emma Hastings, firstborn daughter of Ralph Hastings.
In 1126, at the age of fourteen he married his second cousin same-aged Margaret Stratherne, firstborn daughter of Bartholomew Stratherne, the union lasted 36 years, and 83 days, which resulted in one son and one daughter; their first child, Avice, 1st Princess was born in 1149, only their other child, Ralph II survived. In 1162, at the age of fifty-one he married his second cousin thirty-nine-year-old Isabella Stratherne, third daughter of Bartholomew Stratherne, the union lasted four years, and 331 days, which resulted in one daughter; their only child, Alviva, 2nd Princess was born in 1166, but she didn't survive.
In 1114 he ascended the throne at the age of three and reigned the next 52 years and 196 days. Jocelyn V was the 6th ruler from Braintree dynasty.

Ralph II (1150–1200)

1167–1200

  • Son of Jocelyn V

In 1167, at the age of sixteen he married his second cousin fourteen-year-old Mabel Seaton, fourth daughter of William Seaton, the union lasted ten years, and 33 days, which resulted in one son and one daughter; their first child, Alviva, 1st Princess was born in 1172, the surviving children were: Alviva and Jocelyn VI. In 1177, at the age of twenty-six he married his second cousin thirty-five-year-old Edith Seaton, firstborn daughter of William Seaton, the union lasted 17 years, and 253 days, which resulted in one son and one daughter; their first child, Edith, 2nd Princess was born in 1180, alas no issue survived. In 1195, at the age of forty-four he married sixteen-year-old Edith Stratherne, sixth daughter of Nicholas Stratherne, the union lasted five years, and 313 days, which resulted in one son and one daughter; their first child, Matilda, 3rd Princess was born in 1196, the surviving children were: Matilda and William.
In 1167 he ascended the throne at the age of sixteen and reigned the next 33 years and 257 days. Ralph II was the 7th ruler from Braintree dynasty.

In 1236, at the age of fifty-eight he married thirty-six-year-old Alice Hartland, youngest daughter of Hugo Hartland, the union lasted four years, and 166 days, which resulted in one daughter; their only child, Matilda, 1st Princess was born in 1237.
In 1200 he ascended the throne at the age of twenty-three and reigned the next 39 years and 198 days. Jocelyn VI was the 8th ruler from Braintree dynasty.

Bartholomew I (1210–1271)

Osgood (1298–1353)

1325–1353

  • Grandson of Bartholomew I, through his son, Osgood, 6th Prince
  • First cousin of Aylwin I

Osgood was born in 1 April 1298 as the firstborn son of forty-year-old Osgood Wigan, sixth son of Bartholomew I and twenty-year-old Matilda Wimborne, second daughter of Adam Wimborne.
In 1316, at the age of eighteen he married his second cousin seventeen-year-old Alviva Leeds, second daughter of Osgood Leeds, the union lasted 37 years, and 11 days, which resulted in seven sons and ten daughters; their first child, Ralph III was born in 1319, the surviving children were: Ralph III, Aylwin, Alviva, Matilda, Avice, Hugo, William, Alan, Isabella, Edith, and Emma.
In 1325 he ascended the throne at the age of twenty-seven and reigned the next 28 years and 54 days. Osgood was the founder of long-lasting Wigan dynasty.
