Godwin I (965–1019)

    1000–1019

    • Elected

    He ascended the throne at the age of thirty-four and reigned the next 19 years and 72 days.

    Elias I (1004–1054)

    1019–1054

    • Son of Godwin I

    He was born to thirty-nine-year-old Godwin I and thirty-two-year-old Alice. He ascended the throne at the age of fifteen and reigned the next 34 years and 322 days.

    Godwin II (1024–1085)

    1054–1085

    • Son of Elias I

    He was born to twenty-year-old Elias I, youngest son of Godwin I and nineteen-year-old Osanna Tottenham, youngest daughter of Gilbert Tottenham. He ascended the throne at the age of twenty-nine and reigned the next 30 years and 350 days.

    Godwin III (1045–1101)

    1085–1101

    • Son of Godwin II

    He was born to twenty-year-old Godwin II, oldest son of Elias I and thirty-six-year-old Gunnora Home, youngest daughter of Richeman Home. He ascended the throne at the age of thirty-nine and reigned the next 16 years and 254 days.

    Godwin IV (1083–1132)

    1101–1132

    • Son of Godwin III

    He was born to thirty-eight-year-old Godwin III, only son of Godwin II and twenty-eight-year-old Gunnora Home, second daughter of Wymon Home. He ascended the throne at the age of eighteen and reigned the next 30 years and 268 days.

    Godwin V (1122–1182)

    1132–1182

    • Son of Godwin IV

    He was born to thirty-nine-year-old Godwin IV, only son of Godwin III and thirty-nine-year-old Christina Rosebery, oldest daughter of Jocelyn Rosebery. He ascended the throne at the age of nine and reigned the next 49 years and 261 days.

    Godwin VI (1156–1202)

    1182–1202

    • Son of Godwin V

    Elias II (1170–1221)

    1202–1221

    • Son of Godwin V
    • Brother of Godwin VI

    Letard (1174–1226)

    1221–1226

    • Patrilineal 5th generation descendant of Godwin III, through his daughter, Idonea, 3rd Princess

    He was born to thirty-five-year-old Letard Hessle, oldest son of Letard Hessle and twenty-six-year-old Runild Halewood, youngest daughter of Ringer Halewood. He ascended the throne at the age of forty-seven and reigned the next four and 347 days.

    Guy I (1182–1233)

    1226–1233

    Guy II (1208–1274)

    1233–1274

    • Son of Guy I

    Guy III (1263–1313)

    1274–1313

    • Son of Guy II

    Gilbert I (1278–1330)

    1313–1330

    Gilbert III (1340–1362)
